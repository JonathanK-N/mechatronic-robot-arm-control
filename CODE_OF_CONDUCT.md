## üë• Community Roles and Responsibilities

### Project Maintainers

**Responsibilities:**
- Enforce the Code of Conduct fairly and consistently
- Review and merge contributions that meet quality standards
- Provide technical guidance and mentorship
- Maintain project security and intellectual property protection
- Ensure compliance with industrial standards

**Authority:**
- Remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, and other contributions
- Ban temporarily or permanently any contributor for inappropriate behavior
- Make final decisions on technical architecture and implementation

### Contributors

**Responsibilities:**
- Follow the Code of Conduct in all interactions
- Respect intellectual property and licensing terms
- Maintain high technical standards in contributions
- Provide constructive feedback and peer review
- Report violations of the Code of Conduct

**Rights:**
- Fair consideration of technical contributions
- Respectful treatment regardless of background
- Access to project resources and documentation
- Recognition for valuable contributions

### Industrial Partners

**Special Considerations:**
- **Confidentiality Agreements**: May be required for certain contributions

- **Safety Validation**: Additional review for safety-critical components

## üö® Enforcement Procedures

### Reporting Violations

**Primary Contact**: conduct@mechatronic-solution.com  
**Anonymous Reporting**: Available through project website  
**Emergency Contact**: +1 (514) 123-4567 (for safety-related issues)

### Investigation Process

1. **Initial Assessment** (24 hours)
   - Acknowledge receipt of report
   - Preliminary severity assessment
   - Assign investigation team

2. **Investigation** (72 hours)
   - Gather relevant information
   - Interview involved parties
   - Consult with legal/HR if necessary

3. **Resolution** (1 week)
   - Determine appropriate action
   - Implement corrective measures
   - Communicate outcome to reporter

### Enforcement Actions

| Severity | Violation Type | Action | Duration |
|----------|----------------|--------|----------|
| **Minor** | Unprofessional language | Warning + guidance | - |
| **Moderate** | Repeated minor violations | Temporary suspension | 1-4 weeks |
| **Serious** | Harassment, discrimination | Permanent ban | Indefinite |
| **Critical** | Safety violations, IP theft | Legal action + ban | Permanent |

### Appeal Process

Contributors may appeal enforcement actions by:
1. Submitting written appeal to appeals@mechatronic-solution.com
2. Providing additional context or evidence
3. Requesting review by independent panel
4. Final decision within 30 days

### Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA)

Contributors working with proprietary components must:
- Sign appropriate confidentiality agreements
- Understand IP ownership and licensing terms
- Respect trade secret protections
- Follow secure development practices
